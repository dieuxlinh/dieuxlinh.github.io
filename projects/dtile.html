Course Summary

Design Thinking and Innovation in Leadership for Engineers (DTILE) is a course offered at MIT under the Gordon Engineering Leadership (GEL) Program, offered for select MIT, Wellesley, and Harvard students

The course is split into two halves. The first half guides students through design principles that are applicable to any product, system ADD MORE The second half, students apply these principles to create their own voice AI systems with Amazon Alexas. DTILE is the reason why I have one of the projects I'm most proud of, LittleListener.
