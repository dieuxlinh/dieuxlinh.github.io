this, besides being a glimpse into my thoughts, is inspired by two of the few constants in my life: we're not really strangers, and J.

we're not really strangers has made me realize the power of coming curious, and how it often, if not always, leads to leaving connected. i aspire to do so in every relationship i harbor through the years.

J once reminded me that for the first part of our lives, we are judged by the questions we can answer; for the latter, we are judged by the questions we ask. J is the one person, besides myself, who asks me questions i need to grow. i hope you all have a J in your lives, for i'm lucky to have mine.
