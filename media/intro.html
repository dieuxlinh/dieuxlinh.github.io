welcome to eighty eight

an interactive auditory tool that visually represents the piano's eighty eight keys

long story short:
when i hear music, i still see colour.

short story long:
before i could speak in full sentences, my mum was already teaching me piano. she stuck little coloured stickers on every key of her old upright yamaha — the one she grew up with in the countrysides of hanoi — and colour-coded every note on every page of music. she turned sound into something i could see, long before i understood what i was hearing. so i guess the piano became my first love.
like most first loves, life got in the way — and in this case, i let life get in the way.
i played obsessively for years, then walked away 'for good', only to find my way back again. and again.

okay i see how this is starting to sound like one of those 'it's complicated' shitty situationships, but i promise it's love. just messy, stubborn, and real.

it's probably hard to understand, but i hope eighty eight at least makes it a little easier to visualise.
